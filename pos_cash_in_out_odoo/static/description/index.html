POS - Point of Sales Cash In and Cash Out Odoo Apps

This odoo apps module allow to use "Put Money In" , "Take Money Out" and "Cash In- cash Out" Statement from point of sales-POS cash counter/register in Odoo.

POS Cash-In/Cash-Out Configuration

For allow cash-in and cash-out in POS you need to tick "Cash Control" in POS.

Opening Balance in Session

User have to set opening balance for point of sale session.

Put Money In(Cash IN)

User can see "Put Money In(Cash IN)" button on pos screen.

On click of button new wizard will open here user can enter amount and reason for put money in and click "Put Money In" button.

Take Money Out(Cash Out)

User can see "Take Money Out(Cash Out)" button on pos screen.

On click of button new wizard will open here user can enter amount and reason for take money out and click "Take Money Out" button.

Cash In-Out Statement

User can view and check Cash in and cash out transaction history from "Cash In-Out Statement" button on POS screen.

User can print cash in-out statement for specific cashier between selected date.

User can also check cash in-out transactions under Point of Sale > Reporting > POS Cash In Out menu.

Cash In-Out Journal Entry

After validating and closing point of sale session journal entry will created and posted for all cash in-out transaction.
